We tried every single to do list app out there and felt there simply had to be a better way to get things done.
We built STAG3S precisely because of the shortcomings of our competitors and our desire to help others achieve their goals.
Say goodbye to annoying notifications that only get in the way and say hello to a smarter way of motivating yourself.
We drew inspiration from the world of professional road cycling and the concept of tour stages. By mapping out your goals and planning out the right strategy we will assist you every step of the way so you won't feel as alone in your endeavor.

If you are simply looking for an app to remind you to pick up a gallon of milk on your way home then this is not the app for you.
We want to help change lives and we can use all the help we can get.
